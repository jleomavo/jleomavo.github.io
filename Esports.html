What started off as a hobby quickly became an integral part of my life. My name is Jose but in Gears of War, I am widely known as FraNChiS

My Esports Journey

During my Esports career, I have traveled across the United States, Mexico, Canada, England and France to compete. I have won multiple championships in multiple Gears of War titles and I am the only Gears of War Esports player to place top 3 in every Gears of War title. I have played under many top-tier organizations, professional athletes and sponsors such as Envy, Echo Fox, Rise, GFuel, SCUF, HyperX and Monster.

Thanks to UMG and The Coalition, I was heavily involved in the operations of the Gears Esports program, Lady Spartan Pro Series (Halo) and Apex Arenas. This role included, but was not limited to, community management, initiative planning, social media management, client relations, tournament operations, and overseeing sponsoship programs.

As of May 15th, 2021, I retired as a professional gamer.
